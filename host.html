Vacation, Meet Home

For many, vacations can be more stressful than just staying home. Everyone deserves a break now and then.

Host provides that much needed break.

Travel to your favorite destination, go inside, and let someone else take care of you.

It's that simple.

Who

Host is for anyone wanting to get away and vacation worry-free

What

Users explore all available trips, book one, and the Host does the rest

When

Anytime a user can use their phone, and wants to get away

Where

Anywhere a user may use their phone, Wiﬁ not included

Why

Helping everyone to take time for themselves, one trip at a time

User Flow

Compared to other home-sharing apps on the market, Host enlists a simpliﬁed user ﬂow. The linear paths to listing and booking help keep the user focus on vacation.

Get in, find your perfect spot, and book it.

Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Wireframes were designed to keep the user along a linear path, just as the user ﬂow demonstrates. Creating a layout that was simpliﬁed yet effective was the main objective when wireframing.
