About

My Story

My name is Jordan Brown, and I am a UI designer based out of Kansas City, KS. I have a strong passion for problem solving, helping others, and creating designs that are visually exciting with simple functionality.

My interest in UI design came from a desire to have an outlet to express myself creatively through visuals. After researching possible options that would help me achieve not only the design aspect, but more importantly the ability to help others in achieving their goals, UI design clicked.

In my free time, I enjoy making music, reading books, woodworking, and spending time with the ones that I love.
